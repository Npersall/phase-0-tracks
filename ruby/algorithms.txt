Elevator algorithm:
move on to elevator

locate control panel on left side of door
If control panel is not on left side of door move to right

end

-select floor needed by pushing button of floor marked with a number

if floor needed is current floor, leave elevator
-otherwise choose floor

-wait for elevator to stop moving,doors to open 
-step left and leave elevator
end


How to start a car: 
    Move to the front left side of vehicle

    pull on handle and swing open door
    if locked, use remote to unlock.
    otherwise, proceed to sit down in seat

    put your foot on left pedal and hold

    with your right hand insert key into ignition
    turn key right until car starts up
    let go of key
end
